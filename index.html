Introduction
📘

Copper Guard is a protection device designed to prevent copper wire theft and ensure the safety of telecom infrastructure. This guide provides simple, step-by-step instructions for installation and setup.

4
Installation Instructions
🔧
1
Step 1 — Connect Copper Wires
  • Attach the copper wires to the device
  • Carefully match the + (positive) and – (negative) terminals
  • Follow the sticker labels on the device to ensure correct connection
2
Step 2 — Connect Power Supply
  • Use the provided 5V 2A wall adapter
  • Plug it into the correct power port (as indicated by the device sticker)
  • Switch on the power
✅
The Power LED will light up in blue, confirming the device is powered correctly.
5
Device Setup via SMS
💬
STEP 1 Initialize Connection

Send an SMS to the device SIM number:

connect

You will receive a reply SMS confirming the connection.

STEP 2 Configure Settings

After receiving the reply, configure the following:

Assign names to the 4 wire inputs
Enable or disable each wire as required
Add an Emergency contact number
Add an SMS notification number
